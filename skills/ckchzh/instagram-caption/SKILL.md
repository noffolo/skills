## 专业建议

- Reels > everything** for reach — Instagram is pushing short-form video hard
- Saves and Shares** are the strongest engagement signals (more than likes)
- Carousel posts** get re-shown to followers who didn't swipe all slides
- First comment** with hashtags works just as well as inline hashtags
- Consistency** matters more than frequency — 3-5 posts/week is ideal
